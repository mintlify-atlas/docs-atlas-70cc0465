## What is Virasat?

**Virasat** (विरासत — meaning "heritage" in Hindi) is an interactive web platform that brings India's cultural legacy to life. Built on NCERT curriculum content, it lets students, educators, and culture enthusiasts explore the traditions, monuments, art forms, and history of every Indian state and union territory.

The platform combines a D3-powered interactive map, a rich art gallery, a chronological timeline, 3D hand gesture models, educational games, and a curated glossary — all in one immersive experience.

## Key highlights

- **All 28 states + 8 union territories** covered with cultural data sourced from NCERT textbooks
- **50 interactive 3D Hasta Mudra models** — rotate and zoom each hand gesture in your browser
- **7 educational mini-games** including drag-and-drop state mapping and culture trivia — with downloadable completion certificates
- **Classical dance forms** documented with Navrasas (9 emotional expressions) and gesture details
- **Regional navigation** — filter by North, South, East, West, Central, Northeast, and Union Territories
- **11-language selector** in the navbar — English, Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, Urdu, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi (Bhashini API integration)
- **Dark glassmorphism UI** with smooth Framer Motion animations
